HyperFlow Protocol is a next-generation DeFi infrastructure platform designed specifically for the HyperEVM ecosystem. By combining intelligent yield optimization, cross-protocol liquidity aggregation, and native HyperCore integration, HyperFlow establishes the foundational infrastructure for advanced DeFi operations on HyperEVM.
